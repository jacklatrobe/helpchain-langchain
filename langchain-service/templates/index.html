Welcome to HelpChain

HelpChain is a novel software solution that utilizes large language models and advanced natural language processing techniques to enable semantic interactions with the software.
The solution leverages OpenAI, LangChain and more to combine some powerful components into a VERY powerful AI.
The key feature of HelpChain is its ability to chain multiple queries together to produce more complex results and perform research.

This means you can ask more complex, multi-stage questions like:

  • "What is the current weather in the hometown of steven hawking raised to the power of 8?"
  • "Summarise todays news headlines from the Australian Financial Review, specifying ASX codes for any companies mentioned, in a well formatted list"
  • "As the CTO of a large enterprise, my teams are having trouble locating the information they need in our knowledgebase. How could the tool described on this web page help: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/search/search-what-is-azure-search"
